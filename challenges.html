Understanding Urban Environmental Challenges

Cities face numerous environmental challenges that affect the quality of life of their residents. By visualizing these challenges, we can better understand where nature-based solutions can have the most significant impact. Use the interactive map above to explore different environmental factors in urban areas.

  • Air Pollution: Areas with high levels of air pollutants, which can be mitigated through urban forests and green corridors.
  • Urban Heat Islands: Locations experiencing higher temperatures due to urban development, which can be cooled through green spaces and water features.
  • Flood Risk Areas: Zones prone to flooding, where nature-based solutions like rain gardens and permeable surfaces can help manage stormwater.
  • Existing Green Spaces: Current green areas in the city, which can be expanded or connected to create a more robust urban ecosystem.
