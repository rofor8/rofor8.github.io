Our Services

Comprehensive Solutions for Urban Sustainability

Spatial Analysis

Our advanced spatial analysis service utilizes cutting-edge GIS technology and data analytics to identify optimal locations for implementing nature-based solutions in urban environments. We consider multiple factors including existing green spaces, population density, air quality, and more to provide a comprehensive understanding of where interventions will have the greatest impact.

Urban Planning Consultation

Our team of experienced urban planners and environmental scientists provides expert consultation on integrating green spaces and nature-based solutions into urban development projects. We work closely with city officials, developers, and community stakeholders to create sustainable, resilient urban designs that enhance both environmental quality and citizens' quality of life.

NBS Implementation

We don't just stop at planning - our NBS Implementation service takes your project from concept to reality. Our team manages the entire process of bringing nature-based solutions to life in urban areas, including project management, coordination with contractors, and ensuring compliance with local regulations. We specialize in a variety of NBS including green roofs, urban forests, rain gardens, and more.

Community Engagement

Successful urban transformation requires buy-in from local communities. Our Community Engagement service focuses on involving residents, local businesses, and other stakeholders in the planning and implementation process. We organize workshops, conduct surveys, and facilitate discussions to ensure that our nature-based solutions align with community needs and aspirations.
